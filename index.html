Frequently Asked Questions

What is the delivery time? ➕

We offer same day deliveries for orders confirmed before 1pm! For orders placed after 1pm, delivery will be the following day. You can track your order in real-time once it's dispatched!

What payment methods do you accept? ➕

We accept M-Pesa, Credit/Debit Cards, and Cash on Delivery for your convenience!

How do I earn points? ➕

You earn 1 point for every 100 KSH spent! Sign up now and get 100 welcome points. Points can be redeemed for discounts!

Do you offer gift cards with messages? ➕

Yes! Add a gift card with a personalized message for just KSH 80 during checkout. Perfect for special occasions! 💌
